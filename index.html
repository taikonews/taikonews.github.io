By the People for the People

As the divide between Journalists and Readers keeps growing, Taiko News aims not just to be another political news aggregator. Instead we are a global community where readers regain control of the news.

At Taiko News we want you to become part of the new reporting process, so our platform empowers readers to rate the political bias of articles. Through this readers signal their desire for unbiased reporting from any and all news outlets.
Our platform is not limited to rating politically bias articles, instead we allow our community volunteers to assign content tags to articles, which helps the community better understand individual articles.

Through your participation, not only can you clearly understand relevant articles but contribute your knowledge for the benefit of a wider global community.
